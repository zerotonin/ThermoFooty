# ThermoFooty Documentation

**Pre-registered natural-experiment test of the heat-aggression
hypothesis on European soccer.** Player aggression (red cards for
violent conduct) and crowd violence (football-related arrests) as a
function of the day-of-match temperature anomaly at the stadium,
across the Big-5 European leagues 1970–2026 and the FIFA World Cup +
UEFA Euro + Copa América + AFCON + AFC Asian Cup tournament panel.

The full design is locked at OSF
([10.17605/OSF.IO/YZVAK](https://doi.org/10.17605/OSF.IO/YZVAK))
with an AsPredicted one-pager cross-post for the H1 confirmatory
test ([aspredicted.org/av2un9.pdf](https://aspredicted.org/av2un9.pdf)).

ThermoFooty is one chapter of the lab's cross-species heat-aggression
programme — see also
[ThermoStrife](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20371612)
(human historical-uprisings track) and
[ThermoKourt](https://github.com/zerotonin/thermokourt)
(*Drosophila* behavioural-arena track).
